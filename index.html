The Rock

The Rock is a chill Jamaican jerk food restaurant located in Brownsville. Usually a pretty cool place to hang out, grab some pretty affordable and filling meals. My personal favorite is rice and peas with barbecue chicken, jerk chicken and ox tail gravy.
